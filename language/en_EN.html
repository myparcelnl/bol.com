Manual Bol.com


Introduction

Use our Bol.com integration and save time when creating your shipping labels. You can choose to import your orders manually at the click of a button or have them imported automatically.

1. Installation

1.1 New installation

In your backoffice, go to > Account settings > Integration > External links > Bol.com and click the button. You will be directed to Bol.com where you will need to log in. Then click ‘Allow’ to create the link between MyParcel and Bol.com.

1.2 Updating an existing installation

When a new version of the link is launched, you will use it straight away. You do not need to perform updates yourself.

1.3 Frequently asked questions about the installation

Can I withdraw my permission to link MyParcel to Bol.com?

You can undo the link between your Bol.com account and MyParcel. To do so, click the -button. This will become visible after installation through > Account settings > Integration > External links > Bol.com.

2. Configuratie

2.1 Importinstellingen

You can import orders manually or automatically using the link. Go to > Account settings > Integration > External links > Bol.com to configure this.

2.2 Pakketvoorkeuren

The bol.com link uses your standard parcel preferences when importing Bol.com orders. Go to > Shop settings > Shipments. Under ‘Parcel preferences’, you can select a standard parcel type and any shipping options.

2.3 Frequently asked questions about the configuration

How often do you retrieve new orders if I switch on the automatic imports option?

We retrieve new pending orders every five minutes.

Can I still retrieve orders manually if I configure the import settings to ‘automatic’?

Yes, you can still retrieve orders manually.

Can I link multiple Bol.com shops to my account in MyParcel?

It is possible to link 1 Bol.com account to 1 MyParcel 'sub'shop. To link a new Bol.com shop, create a new subshop in your MyParcel-account.

3. Use of plugin

3.1 Creating one or more shipping labels manually

Once you have installed the Bol.com link, the button will be visible in the shipments summary. Click here to view your pending orders in Bol.com. If you have installed multiple external links, you can switch between the various tabs. On this page, you can choose to import orders individually or in bulk.

3.2 Making changes to one shipment

Imported orders are created as a draft shipment. Click next to the shipment in order to proceed to processing.

3.3 3.3 FAQs about using the plug-in

Do you send the barcode to Bol.com?

Yes. Once the barcode has been created it is sent to Bol.com. The order status then changes to ‘Complete’.

Can I configure parcel preferences for individual order imports?

No. Unfortunately you can’t set parcel preferences for importing individual orders. The standard parcel preferences configured will be used for each import.
